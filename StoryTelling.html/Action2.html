Story Telling Through Action

Dragon Ball Z

In the 1995 film "Dragon Ball Z: Fusion Reborn" Goku and his hardheaded companion Vegeta get sent to the under world to defeat the Demon "Janemba" because he is causing mayhem and destruction in the under world where he has trapped king "Yemma". While Goku is holding off the demon for a limited amount of time, his friend "Vegeta" arrives on time to help.
